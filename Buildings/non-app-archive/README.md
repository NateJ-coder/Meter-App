# Non-App Archive

This folder contains files moved out of `Buildings/buildings` during the meter-app cleanup pass.

## Why These Files Were Moved

The retained source tree is intended to focus on:

- building identity and building-specific reference material
- meter inventories and meter identifiers
- electricity reading sheets, photos, and related evidence
- files likely to help build an audit trail of historical electricity readings

The archived files were moved here because they were classified as one of the following:

- administrative or financial statements and invoices
- payment proofs and account-arrears artifacts
- proxy letters, authority letters, negotiations, and legal/admin correspondence
- council reconciliation folders not directly needed for the electricity-reading audit trail app
- other office or mail artifacts without a clear meter-reading or building-data signal
